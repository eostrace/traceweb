Supply Chain Collaboration

The data on the blockchain can be efficiently shared among different departments of the supply chain, Achieve unified voucher, Whole process record.

  • Effectively solve the problems of multi-party participation, information fragmentation, repeated review of circulation links and so on.
  • Timely and efficient, reduce logistics costs and improve efficiency.

Efficient Regulation

From production to sales, the subject of each link writes the information signature into the EOS blockchain with its own identity (private key). The information cannot be tampered with and the identity cannot be denied.

If there is a dispute, we can quickly locate the problem link, so as to provide evidence and pursue responsibility.
